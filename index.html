About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of South Australia. I have a Bachelor of Information Technology, majoring in Games and Entertainment Design. I like to draw inspiration from some of my favourite video games like Titanfall 2, Celeste and Subnautica. I love their unique movement mechanics and engaging storytelling. I someday hope to create a video game that I believe is to same standard as these games and gives players the same joy that I get from playing video games.
